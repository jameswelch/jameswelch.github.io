Multibrot #30 is an art series where a single piece of onchain art will be updated weekly for a year. The original piece is Multibrot #30, an NFT on the Ethereum blockchain, part of the Brotchain series by Divergance and Harri.

Brotchain is a generative NFT art series, with bitmaps generated and rendered entirely by a blockchain contract. No external code is needed, just 100% Solidity.

66 backers helped raise funds to pay for the onchain updates to the piece, and will be sent an NFT summarising the changes over time at the end of the project.

Most interestingly, owners can use a flexible colour editor to preview changes in a Brot’s colour scheme, then update their piece with an Ethereum transaction.

Brotchain was created by Divergence and Harri. Divergence is known for their previous series, PROOF OF {ART}WORK (check it out, and don’t forget to zoom).
